Ethereal Pop Mart Blind Boxes

Nyota: Genesis

1/12 Chance

Genesis is meant to signify the start of a something new. Its composition between Nyota and the cat create a sense of harmony, paired with their colors creates a sense of fresh beginnings and innoncence. This blindbox is pure whimsy and the best in the series.

Hirono: Terracotta Army

1/7 Chance

This special edition collaboration series represents Chinese culture. The Terracotta warrior figure brings a sense of history home to you in this blindbox. It captures so many details and even has a stone effect to replicate the real warriors.

1001 Moons: Midsommar Drift

1/12 Chance

This beatiful blind box represents a fish in a bag. This is one of the most unique and beautifully executed blind boxes that uses transparency and colors to feel like you're really holding a bag of water.

Dimoo: Propitious Jades

1/12 Chance

This figure is meant to depict jade. Through its use of varying shades of blue and green complimented by red it creates a very magical and whimsical feel.

Nyota: Dream

1/12 Chance

This series of blind box focuses on various childhood experiences. My most favorite of the series is Dream. It represents our dreams being high in the sky and still being attainable. The mix of colors and deep meaning create the most lovely blind box for a collector to seek out.

Hirono: The Fox

1/12 Chance

Thia figure perfectly captures the friendship formed between the little and the fox who begged to be domesticated. It perfectly captures the attachment that built between one another and showcases their story in a clear and tragic way.
